Cookie Policy

This page explains how we use cookies on the MPE UK Ltd website.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function, improve security, and (with your consent) provide analytics about how visitors use the site.

Cookies we use

Essential cookies

These are required for the site to work correctly (for example: security, session and form protection). They cannot be switched off.

Analytics / tracking cookies (optional)

If you accept cookies, we use Lead Forensics tracking to understand how visitors navigate the site and which pages are viewed. This helps us improve the website and respond to interest in our products.

Your choices

You can accept or reject non-essential cookies using the cookie banner. If you change your mind later, you can clear your browser cookies for this site and the banner will reappear.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us via the contact page.
